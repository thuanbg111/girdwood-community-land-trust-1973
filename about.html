AboutGirdwood Community Land Trust

Girdwood Community Land Trust is a U.S. 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 10151973) formed to bring practical support to people in transition. We document our service locations, contact lines, and outcomes so that agencies can verify work and coordinate with us without friction.

Our team comes from outreach, workforce, and housing backgrounds. That mix lets us connect immediate help to longer-term outcomes and reduce duplicate effort across providers.
